Pagan and Druid Symbolism

Cross
Christian Use: Symbol of crucifixion (1 Corinthians 1:18, KJV).
Pagan Origin: Egyptian ankh, Celtic cross.

Easter
Christian Use: Resurrection of Christ (John 20:1, KJV).
Pagan Origin: Eostre, fertility festivals.
Biblical Condemnation: Deuteronomy 12:30-31 (KJV).

Christmas
Christian Use: Christ’s birth (Luke 2:7, KJV).
Pagan Origin: Saturnalia, Yule, Sol Invictus.

Halloween
Christian Use: All Hallows’ Eve.
Pagan Origin: Samhain.
Biblical Condemnation: Deuteronomy 18:10-12 (KJV).

Triskele
Christian Use: Symbol of Trinity.
Pagan Origin: Celtic spiral symbol.
Biblical Condemnation: Exodus 20:4 (KJV).
